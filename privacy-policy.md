# Privacy Policy for NeurIPS25

**Last Updated**: November 23, 2025

## Overview

NeurIPS25 is a conference companion app designed to help you navigate the NeurIPS 2025 conference. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

**NeurIPS25 does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data with external servers or third parties.**

All data stays on your device.

## What Data is Stored Locally

The following data is stored **only on your device**:

- **Session Preferences**: Which sessions you've marked as attending
- **Personal Notes**: Your notes and ratings for sessions and papers
- **Meeting Schedules**: Meetings you've scheduled with other attendees
- **Contact Information**: People you've added to your personal contact list
- **App Settings**: Your preferences for the app

## Data Storage

- All data is stored in your device's local storage
- No data is transmitted to any external servers
- No cloud synchronization (unless you use iOS backup)
- No analytics or tracking services

## Data Sharing

- **You control your data**: Export features allow YOU to share your schedule/notes at your discretion
- **No automatic sharing**: We never share your data automatically
- **No third parties**: We don't use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services
- **No user accounts**: The app doesn't require login or account creation

## Data Deletion

You can delete all app data by:

1. Deleting the NeurIPS25 app from your device, or
2. Using the "Clear All Data" options within the app (if available)

All data will be permanently removed from your device.

## iOS Backup

If you use iCloud Backup or iTunes/Finder backup:
- Your app data may be included in your device backup
- This is controlled by iOS system settings, not by our app
- To exclude our app from backups, go to iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage → Backups

## Children's Privacy

NeurIPS25 does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is designed for academic and professional use at a scientific conference.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to external websites (e.g., conference websites, paper repositories). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- You have complete control over your data
- You can access all your data within the app
- You can delete all data by deleting the app
- No data subject access requests are needed (you already have all your data)

## Compliance

This app complies with:
- Apple App Store Guidelines
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - by not collecting any data
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - by not collecting any data
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) - by not collecting any data

**Summary**: NeurIPS25 is a privacy-first app. All your data stays on your device. We don't collect anything.
